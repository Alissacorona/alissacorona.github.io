Annual Bug-Off Tournament

The Tri-Town Annual Bug-Off Tournament

-Start First Week of July-

The Bug-Off Tournament is a Tri-Town tradition that has been around since the 1940's. Take in the beauty of Idaho while catching intersting crawlers. Who ever catches the rare Agrias Butterfly wins not only bragging rights, but a cash prize of $500!
