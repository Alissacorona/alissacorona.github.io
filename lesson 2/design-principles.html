Design Principles

Alissa Corona

Contrast

American Eagle

AmericanEagle.com

I chose this website to show off the contrast of colors. You can see that there is a black and white color contrast there. The contrast makes it easier for the reader’s eye. It looks clean but also professional. I thought this was a good example of contrast since black and white are the opposites of each other.

Visual Hierarchy

Sephora

Sephora.com

I thought the Sephora website was a good example of visual Hierarchy. Starting off your eye is drawn to the header “Just Arrives New Holiday Sets” because it is the biggest font on the page. The website to announce the newest event that is happening. I think this is a smart idea for a business to do if they want to express to the customers the newest thing that is happening.

Alignment

Apple

Apple.com

This Apple website does a good job of alignment. The pyramid of the apps showing off the App Store was a good design idea, because when you keep scrolling the products they want to show off align with the top of the pyramid.
