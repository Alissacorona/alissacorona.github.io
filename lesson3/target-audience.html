Target Audiance

Purpose of a target audience is to create the website with their needs in mind so if will attract people.

Persona 1

Persona: Mom (Jane Lane)

Jane Lane is a hardworking single mom with four kids between the ages of 5-15. She takes them to school and to their after-school activities: like soccer practice and mutual. Sticking to the schedule is very important to Jane. She plans out everything and does not leave out any details, especially what the weather is going to be like.

Scenarios

  • Jane’s son and daughter have out of town soccer tournaments. She needs to use the Over the Rain WX to know what the weather is going to be like so she can pack accordingly.
  • Jane wants to get her car washed in between dropping her kids off at actives and picking them up. She wants to see if it is going to rain that week.
  • On Sunday Jane’s family wants to go on a walk in the mountains. Jane needs to check the weather to see if they family needs to bring a jacket if it gets too cold.
  • Jane and her family want to have a campfire that night. Jane needs to know if it is going to be too windy to build a fire.

Persona 2

Persona: Athlete (Nikita Skipper)

Nikita is a serious athlete. From running six miles a day to biking in the mountains all day. She also likes to camp in the mountains during the weekends. She loves to work out, especially outside. She likes to know what the weather will be like in advance to she can plan her workouts according. But she can also be spontaneous.

Scenarios

  • Nikita wants to go camping in the Teton forest this weekend. She needs to know what the weather will be like so she can know how intense she needs to pack for the weekend.
  • Nikita is trying to train for the Ironman competition coming up. She wants to run outside so she gets used to running on different terrain. She needs to know what the weather is going to be like so she can dress according. She does not care if it will rain.
  • Nikita wants to plan a hiking trip with her roommates. But her roommates will not go unless the weather is not too hot to hike. She needs to know when the best day will be to go so her roommates will go with her.
